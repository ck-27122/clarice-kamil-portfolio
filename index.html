About Me

I'm a Vancouver-based graphic designer with a knack for solving problems visually — turning complex ideas into clear, compelling design. Born in the Philippines, raised in Canada, and shaped by both.

I completed my Graphic Design certificate at BCIT in 2025, and in the two years since I started designing I've worked across brand identity, editorial layout, and web design. I also built this portfolio by hand — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — because I believe understanding how design lives on screen makes me a better designer.

My work leans toward brand identity, but I'm drawn to any problem where visual systems need to do real work — whether that's a logo, a menu, or a digital experience. I want to be in an agency environment where I can keep learning fast and work on things that matter.
